Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Currently, donations to ACloudViewer are not tax-deductible as we are not a registered non-profit organization. We are working towards establishing a foundation that would allow for tax-deductible contributions.
Can I donate anonymously?
Yes, absolutely! If you prefer to remain anonymous, simply let us know in your donation message or email, and we will respect your privacy.
Will I receive a receipt?
Yes, we can provide a receipt for your donation upon request. Please contact us at contact@acloudviewer.com with your donation details.
How can my company sponsor ACloudViewer?
We offer special corporate sponsorship packages with various benefits including logo placement, priority support, and custom feature development. Please contact us at contact@acloudviewer.com to discuss options.
Can I contribute in other ways?
Yes! Besides financial support, you can contribute code, documentation, bug reports, feature suggestions, or help others in our community forums. Every contribution is valuable!
